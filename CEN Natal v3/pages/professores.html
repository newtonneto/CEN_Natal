Nome: Frink

Cor do cabelo: Marrom

Ocupação: Professor, Inventor

Biografia: Born in the 1950s, Frink is Springfield's local mad scientist. He has a trademark mannerism of using Tourettes-like gibberish when excited, such as "GLAYVIN!" and shouting other words that have no relevance to the situation at hand. He also occasionally refers to the importance of remembering to "carry the one" in various mathematical calculations. He is almost never seen without his glasses and has only taken them off once.
